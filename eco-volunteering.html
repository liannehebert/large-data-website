Eco-Volunteering

Take part in one of GoEco's exciting and unique volunteer opportunities in South America! From the street lights of Buenos Aires to the Andes Mountains in Argentina, we offer a wide array of diverse options for volunteers interested in South America. Get to know a Bolivian family, make friends with travelers from around the world in Patagonia, or learn about conservation on the Galapagos Islands - with GoEco you can do it all!

Argentina - Spanish Language and Community Projects in Córdoba

Join one of three projects in the cultural capital of the Americas, Córdoba, Argentina. Immerse yourself in Argentinian culture and improve your Spanish, all while volunteering with children, animals, or the elderly.

Location Córdoba, Argentina
Project Length Min 4 weeks — max 12 weeks
Volunteer Work Community center, animal shelter, or home for the elderly
Age 18+
Volunteer in Córdoba

Argentina - Community work in Buenos Aires and Patagonia

Join this project in vibrant Buenos Aires to help out in a variety of community settings. Volunteers have the opportunity to immerse themselves in Argentinian culture, while making a lasting contribution to the quality of life for children and the general community. Split your time between Buenos Aires and Patagonia for a varied experience.

Location Buenos Aires & Patagonia, Argentina
Project Length Min 2 weeks — max 12 weeks
Volunteer Work Community work with children, youth and adults
Number of Participants 10 International volunteers
Age 18-50
Volunteer in Buenos Aires

Bolivia - Orphan Care, Teaching, and Spanish Immersion

Improve your Spanish and volunteer in Bolivia's capital city, Sucre, with vulnerable children in either an orphanage or kindergarten placement. Help secure the future of these children and experience Bolivian culture with volunteers from around the world.

Location Sucre, Bolivia
Project Length Min 2 weeks — max 8 weeks
Volunteer Work Informal teaching, work with children
Number of Participants Up to 3 international volunteers
Age 18-70
Volunteer in Bolivia

Peru - Helping Underprivileged Children in Cusco

Travel to beautiful Peru to improve your Spanish and volunteer with underprivileged children in either a health centre, a home for children in need, a youth rehabilitation center, or a kindergarten. All these projects are in Cusco - a World Heritage Site, and the historic capital of the Inca Empire.

Location Cusco, Peru
Project Length Min 4 weeks — max 8 weeks
Volunteer Work Working with children
Age 18-70
Volunteer in Peru
